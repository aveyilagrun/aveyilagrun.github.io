About Me

I am currently a Software Development Engineer at Autodesk. I hold a BEng in Electronic & Electrical Engineering from University College London and an MSc in Computing from Imperial College London. My work and personal interests span sustainable energy systems and exploring the intricacies of the human brain. Recently, I've been fascinated by how large language models and their active use in agentic workflows are shaping how people approach problem solving. I am passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful impact. When I'm not coding, I enjoy reading, exploring new ideas and crafting DIY projects.

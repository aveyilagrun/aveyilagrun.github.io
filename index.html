About Me

Currently working as a technology consultant at Autodesk. With a BEng in Electronic & Electrical Engineering from University College London and an MSc in Computing from Imperial College London, I specialise in creating innovative tech solutions. My interests include sustainable energy systems and exploring the intricacies of the human brain. I'm dedicated to leveraging technology to make a positive impact on the world. When I'm not coding, you can find me reading something from my reading list or spending time with my loved ones.
